Join the NHS Wales Solutions Exchange

Get involved with the GitHub GIG Cymru community and start contributing to innovative healthcare solutions across Wales.

The GitHub GIG Cymru (GitHub Interest Group Wales) is a community of developers, healthcare professionals, and technology enthusiasts working together to improve healthcare through innovative digital solutions.

Who Can Join?
  • NHS Wales Staff: Employees of NHS Wales organizations and health boards
  • Healthcare Professionals: Clinicians, nurses, allied health professionals working in Wales
  • Technology Teams: Developers, data scientists, and IT professionals in Welsh healthcare
  • Academic Partners: Researchers and students from Welsh universities working on healthcare technology
  • Public Sector: Local government and public health professionals in Wales
What You'll Get Access To
  • Access to NHS Wales GitHub organizations and repositories
  • Collaboration opportunities with healthcare technology teams
  • Training and development resources
  • Community support and mentorship
  • Early access to new tools and platforms
  • Networking opportunities with like-minded professionals
Prerequisites
  • Valid NHS Wales email address or academic/public sector email in Wales
  • GitHub account (free account is sufficient)
  • Basic understanding of version control or willingness to learn
  • Commitment to collaboration and knowledge sharing

Follow these steps to become part of the GitHub GIG Cymru community:

Step 1: Prepare Your GitHub Account
  1. Create a GitHub account at github.com if you don't have one
  2. Add your NHS Wales or institutional email to your GitHub account
  3. Complete your GitHub profile with your name and professional information
  4. Enable two-factor authentication for security
Step 2: Submit Your Application

Complete the application process by providing:

  • Your GitHub username
  • NHS Wales or institutional email address
  • Your role and organization within NHS Wales
  • Brief description of your interest in the community
  • Any relevant technical skills or experience
Step 3: Wait for Approval

Our team will review your application and:

  • Verify your NHS Wales or institutional affiliation
  • Send you an invitation to join relevant GitHub organizations
  • Provide access to community resources and documentation
  • Connect you with relevant teams and projects
Step 4: Get Oriented

Once approved, you'll receive:

  • Welcome email with getting started resources
  • Access to community Slack channels
  • Information about upcoming training sessions
  • List of active projects looking for contributors

Get in touch with the GitHub GIG Cymru team for support, questions, or to begin your application process.

What to Include in Your Email

When contacting us, please include:

  • Subject Line: "GitHub GIG Cymru Application - [Your Name]"
  • Full Name: Your professional name
  • GitHub Username: Your GitHub account username
  • Organization: Your NHS Wales organization or health board
  • Role: Your job title and department
  • Email: Your NHS Wales or institutional email address
  • Interest: Brief description of why you want to join
  • Experience: Any relevant technical experience (optional)
Response Times
  • Initial Response: Within 2-3 business days
  • Application Processing: 1-2 weeks for verification and approval
  • Account Setup: 1-2 business days after approval
  • Training Opportunities: Monthly sessions available
